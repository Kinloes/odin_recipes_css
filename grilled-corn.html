Don't boil your corn on the cob! Grill it with style.

Corn is always a tasty addition to a dinner, but there are better and worse ways to prepare it.

Here at Cooking for Coders, we believe that grilling corn before you shuck it is the tastiest way to cook it!

Grilling corn while it's still wrapped in it's little corn-shell is a great way to add smoky flavor, but wrapping it in foil is also a great option.

  • Ears of Corn
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Aluminum Foil (If shucked)
