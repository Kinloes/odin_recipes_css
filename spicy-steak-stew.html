For a stew that gets you sweating while tasting great, try this recipe!

This simple recipe uses basic ingredients you can find at any grocery store.

With a little bit of patience you can make a delicious and affordable stew that can last for days!

Remember- you don't need to follow our recipe to a T. For example, you can use different peppers if you want the stew to be more or less spicy

Here are the ingredients you'll want for this recipe.

  • Steak
  • Serrano Peppers
  • Onions
  • Potatos
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Celery
  • Garlic Salt
  • Lemon Pepper
  • Chili Powder
